Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2025

1. Overview

ALC & PARTNERS (“we,” “us,” “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our website.

2. Information We Collect

  • Contact Data: Name, email address, and message content you submit via our contact form.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, and analytics (via cookies).

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide legal service estimates.
  • To improve our website, analyze trends, and monitor performance.
  • To send you administrative notifications (not marketing).

4. Cookies & Analytics

We use minimal cookies (e.g., Google Analytics) to understand usage patterns and improve site functionality. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, although this may limit certain features.

5. Data Security

We employ industry-standard measures (HTTPS, TLS, encrypted email) to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure; use discretion when transmitting sensitive documents.

6. Third‑Party Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share data with service providers (e.g., email API) who process information on our behalf under confidentiality agreements.

7. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by emailing contact@alcandpartners.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically; the “Last updated” date will reflect revisions. Continued use of the site after changes indicates your acceptance.
